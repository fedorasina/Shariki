\doxysubsection{Подробное описание}
Represents a hazardous area that causes player death/respawn. 

Creates a trigger zone that respawns the player at their last checkpoint when entered. Requires a Collider component and depends on \doxylink{class_platformers_1_1_coin_game_manager}{Coin\+Game\+Manager} for respawn functionality.
